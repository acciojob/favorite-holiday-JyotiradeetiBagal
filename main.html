Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar is the ideal hill station getaway from Mumbai with its mesmerising waterfalls, old forts, historic temples and long stretches of vibrant strawberry plantations.


Top 5 attractions in the Mahabaleshwar
  1. Wilson Point (Sunrise Point): Wilson Point is the highest point in Mahabaleshwar and has three watchtowers, each opening up to splendid scenic landscapes.
  2. Venna Lake: Built by Shri Appasaheb Maharaj in 1942, Venna Lake stretches across eight kilometers. Tourists come here to enjoy boat rides, horse rides, and snacks at the many eateries.
  3. Pratapgarh Fort: Built by Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Pratapgarh Fort is situated 24 kms from Mahabaleshwar and has witnessed major wars, like the one between Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan.
  4. Lingmala Waterfall: Lingmala attracts thousands of tourists who come to watch the magnificent waterfall, as it merges with the Venna Lake during monsoons.
  5. Elephant Head: A popular tourist spot, people come here to enjoy the unique greenery surrounding the rock formations that accurately resemble an elephant's head.
Must-try foods in the Mahabaleshwar
  • Strawberry Shake - Veg: A thick, creamy and delicious drink made from milk and freshly harvested Strawberries.
  • Piyush - Veg: A thick, creamy beverage made from buttermilk and shrikhand, flavored with saffron and cardamom.
  • Puran Poli - Veg: A sweet flatbread filled with lentil and jaggery paste, often enjoyed during festivals.
  • Bharli Vangi - Veg: Stuffed brinjals (eggplant) cooked with a special blend of Maharashtrian spices.
